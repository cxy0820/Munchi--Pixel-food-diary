# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Munchi is for young women, students, and young professionals who enjoy capturing coffee, milk tea, desserts, meals, and everyday food moments. They use it in casual mobile contexts, often right after taking a photo, and want a playful memory-keeping flow with a cozy game-like collection feeling.

## Product Purpose
Munchi turns food and drink photos into reusable warm 16-bit pixel stickers for a daily journal, calendar memories, a Pixel Dex collection, and scrapbook collage boards. Success means users can quickly create a pixel bite, revisit it visually, and make something charming from their food memories.

## Brand Personality
Warm, tactile, and gentle. The product should feel like a polished digital scrapbook crossed with a cozy item collection, never like a medical or punitive tracking app.

## Anti-references
Avoid clinical trackers, harsh orange dashboards, generic beige-only AI landing pages, cluttered photo editors, and any guilt-based health language.

## Design Principles
- Pixel stickers first: food and drink item icons carry the emotional center of the product.
- Useful before decorative: every control should support recording, reviewing, arranging, or saving memories.
- Collection over measurement: records should feel like saved treasures, not quantified intake.
- Keep the craft tactile: white borders, paper-like surfaces, and small stateful animations should make the app feel touchable.
- English-only product surface: all visible UI, sample data, docs, and feedback text are written in English.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast for core text and controls. Respect reduced motion, avoid color-only status cues, keep touch targets comfortable on phones, and use clear labels for icon buttons and form inputs.
